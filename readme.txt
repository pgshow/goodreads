Editing the Setting.yml file can chang the genre and rating, rating 0.0 means no filter for rating

Then launch the Goodread.exe

The scraper retries 10 times, if a fetch fails, each retry delays 10s.

If your internet is not good, or his website shaking, the bot will retry 10 times and pop EOF error for a link.

If some page failed eventually, you can close the program, and scrape later when the connection become better.
